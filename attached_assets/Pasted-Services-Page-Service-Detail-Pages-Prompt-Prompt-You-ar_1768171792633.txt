Services Page + Service Detail Pages Prompt

Prompt:

You are a senior web copywriter, UX strategist, and digital marketing expert.

Create a Services page for MJ Digital Media, a digital agency that offers Strategic Web Design, SEO, and Digital Marketing services.

The Services page must clearly summarize each service and link to its own dedicated service detail page. Then, create the full content for each service detail page.

Brand & Business Context

Company Name: MJ Digital Media
Industry: Web Design & Digital Marketing
Target Audience:

Small to mid-sized businesses

Startups and growing brands

Business owners who want more leads, traffic, and revenue

Tone & Style:

Professional, modern, and confident

Friendly and approachable

Clear, benefit-driven, and conversion-focused

Part 1: Services Page

Create a main Services page that includes:

A strong hero section (headline, subheadline, CTA)

A short intro explaining how MJ Digital Media helps businesses grow online

A services overview section featuring:

Strategic Web Design

SEO (Search Engine Optimization)

Digital Marketing

For each service on the Services page:

Write a concise, benefits-focused summary

Include a clear CTA link labeled “Learn More”

Assign SEO-friendly URLs that link to each service detail page:

/services/strategic-web-design

/services/seo

/services/digital-marketing

Part 2: Service Detail Pages

Create a full service detail page for each service listed above.

Each service detail page must include:

Hero section with a clear value-driven headline

Overview of the service and who it’s for

Problems this service solves

How MJ Digital Media delivers this service (process or approach)

Key features translated into business benefits

Expected outcomes / results

Why choose MJ Digital Media for this service

Strong CTAs (e.g., “Schedule a Free Consultation”)

SEO & Conversion Guidelines

Use SEO-friendly headings (H1–H3 structure)

Naturally incorporate relevant keywords (web design, SEO services, digital marketing agency, etc.)

Focus on clarity, trust-building, and conversion

Avoid fluff, clichés, and generic marketing language

Output Format

Clearly label each section:

Services Page

Strategic Web Design Page

SEO Services Page

Digital Marketing Services Page

Provide clean, ready-to-publish website copy

Include internal links exactly as specified above
